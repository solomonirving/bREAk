Events

A new era for bREAk, gives an opportunity to fulfil even greater aspirations in delivering a most personal and immersive dining experience.

WELCOME & INTRODUCTION TO bREAk
Add a special touch to your event with a welcome from Executive Chef Peter Ramsay or the bREAk Head Chef. Once your guests have taken a seat, the chef will welcome your party and talk through the bREAk menu.
Q & A SESSION WITH PETER RAMSAY
Chef Peter Ramsay is celebrated all over the world. Enhance your event with guests given an opportunity to ask questions and chat with the chef who guided bREAk to being one of Sacramento's most awarded restaurants.
bREAk MASTERCLASS WITH PETER RAMSAY
Join Peter in bREAk’s Green Room overlooking sparkling Sacramento River for an intimate cooking class. Learn the secrets of bREAk’s award-winning cuisine over a glass of champagne while Peter demonstrates three dishes from your menu. A masterclass not to be missed.
WINE 101
An interactive discussion on how to taste wine with bREAk's award-winning Head Sommelier, Skiwalker Luke. Designed for people who love wine but want to know more. The talk will arm your guests with insider knowledge and valuable skills on how to taste and enjoy wine.
