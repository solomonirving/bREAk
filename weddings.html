Weddings

bREAk welcomes both small and large wedding parties to celebrate in the heart of the Sacramento River. With menu by chef Peter Ramsay and panoramic views from the Sacramento Bridge to the Opera House, bREAk provides an exquisite setting for your most special day.

With two exclusive reception spaces, bREAk can accommodate small receptions for up to 20 guests in the Upper Tower, and larger seated events for 100 guests or elegant cocktail party receptions for up to 180 guests in the Green Room.
