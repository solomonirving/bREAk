About Us

bREAk is one of the country’s most celebrated restaurants; the creation of leading Sacramento restaurant group, and Executive Chef Peter Ramsay.

The restaurant is an organic space reflective of Peter Ramsay's nature inspired cuisine. The interplay of textures and colour brings life and a vibrance that embraces the restaurant’s place in the dress circle of Sacramento. An ode to Sacramento's landscape, from the vast river, to the fast lightrail, every detail from the ground up has been thoughtfully considered.
